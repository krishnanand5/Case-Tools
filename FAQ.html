1) What is the App about?

Smart Parking System is a unique way of making your parking life a lot easier. Sign Up. Select from a list of places that have partnered with us. Pre-Book your slot. Park and Enjoy.

2) How do I pay for my slot?

You can pay online using your Debit card,Credit card or Internet Banking.

3) Is there a cut-off time for my Booking ?

If you don't occupy your slot within 30 minutes of Booking, the slot will get deallocated and your money will be refunded.

4) How long can I book my slot for?

You can book a slot for a maximum of 5 hours and there is a charging mechanism that will be followed post the 5 hour period.The payment slab that will be followed for a period less than 5 hours is as follows :-
